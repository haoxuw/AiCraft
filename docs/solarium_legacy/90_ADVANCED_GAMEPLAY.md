# The game doesn't have a difficulty selection

The game opens as sandbox and no death could happen

Players play the game. By definition, threads needs to be introduced.

We don't put presure to the player, instead they unlock the threads and rewards, after intentionally construct key structures.

## Two main threats -- sky / depth
### Xenos
But they attrack alien spaceships
Building beacons give you various perks, turns into night
Before arriving, 3 suns at different hours cycle, arrives at LCM of the night

### Dragons
Dragons live in the caves of the depth. You may want a dragon egg to hatch, 
or various precious material, but may run into mystic creatures like dragons
You may also unlock them when terraforming goes too deep and wrong
